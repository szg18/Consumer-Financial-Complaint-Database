Consumer Complaint Database for Financial Protection

About the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau

We work hard to make consumer financial markets work for consumers, responsible providers, and the economy as a whole. We want to protect consumers from unfair, deceptive, or abusive practices and take action against companies that break the law. We arm people with the information, tools, and steps that they may need to make smart financial decisions.

About the Consumer Complaint Database

The facts in these complaints may not be verified, but we do give companies the opportunity to confirm they have a commercial relationship with the consumer before we publish the complaint detailed in this database.

OUR GOALS


Empower

We make tools, answer common questions, and give tips to help consumers navigate their financial choices and look for the deal that works best for them.

Enforce

We take actions against predatory practices and companies that violate the law and have already returned millions of dollars to violated customers.

Educate

We encourage and push for financial education from childhood through retirement, publish research, information, and educate finance companies of their responsiblities.

Our Database

This dataset is a collection of complaints from the Consumer Financial Protection's Complaint Database including a range of consumer financial products and services, and then sent to companies for response. The database updates daily, and contains certain information for each complaint, including the source of the complaint, the date of submission, and the company the complaint was sent to in response.
